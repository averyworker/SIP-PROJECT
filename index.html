QR Youth Resources

QR Youth Resources is a website dedicated to helping youths find information in the community such as referral services ranging from private to nonprofit organizations. This tool is operating 24 hours a day every year. QR Youth Resources help youth individuals and families find important information that they may not even know about. This is a project dedicated to providing critical services that can improve and save lives.
